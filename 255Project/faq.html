Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I schedule a test drive?
A: Yes, you can schedule a test drive by contacting our sales team. They will assist you in setting up a convenient time for your test drive.
Q: Do you offer financing options?
A: Yes, we provide financing options for car purchases. Our finance department can guide you through the process and help you choose the best financing option for your needs.
Q: What is the warranty coverage on your cars?
A: Each car comes with a manufacturer warranty. The duration and coverage of the warranty may vary depending on the make and model of the car. Our sales team can provide you with detailed information about the warranty for a specific car.
Q: What types of payment options do you accept?
A: We accept various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and financing options. Please reach out to our sales team for more details.
Q: Can I trade in my current vehicle when purchasing a car from your dealership?
A: Absolutely! We accept trade-ins and offer competitive valuations. Our experts will assess the value of your vehicle to provide you with a fair trade-in offer.
Q: Are your vehicles thoroughly inspected before being sold?
A: Yes, we have a rigorous inspection process for all our vehicles. Our certified technicians conduct comprehensive inspections to ensure that the cars meet high-quality standards before they are made available for sale.
Q: Do you offer after-sales services and maintenance?
A: Yes, we have a dedicated service center to provide after-sales services and maintenance for your purchased vehicle. Our trained technicians can handle regular maintenance, repairs, and other necessary services.
Q: What is the process for purchasing a car from your dealership?
A: Purchasing a car from our dealership is a simple and straightforward process. You can browse our inventory online, visit our showroom to see the cars in person, and work with our sales team to select the right vehicle for you. Once you've chosen a car, our team will assist you with financing, paperwork, and any additional services you may require.
Q: Can I get a vehicle history report for the used cars you sell?
A: Yes, we understand the importance of transparency when it comes to used cars. We provide comprehensive vehicle history reports for all our used cars. These reports include important details such as previous ownership, accident history (if any), service records, and more. You can request a vehicle history report for any car you're interested in.
Q: Do you offer any warranties for the cars you sell?
A: Yes, we offer warranties for added peace of mind. Our new cars come with manufacturer warranties, while our used cars may have available warranty options. The specific warranty coverage and duration depend on factors such as the car's age, mileage, and condition. Our sales team will provide you with detailed information regarding warranty options for the car you choose.
